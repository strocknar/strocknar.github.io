Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Strocknar Development ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the YAWT – Workout Tracker & Log mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

YAWT is designed to work entirely offline. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our servers. All data you enter — workouts, exercise logs, sets, biometric measurements, and app settings — is stored exclusively on your device.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays a banner advertisement on the Home screen, served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve relevant ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Device type, OS version, and app usage information

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us. Google's use of this data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings under Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

3. Data You Export

The App allows you to export your workout history and biometric data as an XLSX file to your device's local storage. We have no access to exported files. You are responsible for any data you choose to share or transfer.

4. Data You Import

You may import a previously exported XLSX file to restore or migrate your data. Imported data is written only to your device's local database. We do not receive or process this data.

5. Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET — Required to load banner advertisements via AdMob.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — Allows AdMob to check network availability before requesting ads.

No other permissions are requested or required.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 13 and older and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

7. Data Retention

All app data resides on your device. You can delete it at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing the App's data through your device settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
