Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Strocknar Development ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the ColorCue mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

ColorCue is designed to work entirely offline. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our servers. All data generated by the App — calibration thresholds, training session records, and app settings — is stored exclusively on your device.

2. Microphone Use

The App uses your device's microphone solely to detect bat contact sounds during training and calibration sessions. Audio is processed in real time on your device to identify impact events. No audio is recorded, stored, or transmitted. Raw audio samples are analyzed momentarily in memory and immediately discarded.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve relevant ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Device type, OS version, and app usage information

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us. Google's use of this data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings under Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

4. Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions:

  • RECORD_AUDIO — Required to capture microphone input for impact detection during training and calibration.
  • WAKE_LOCK — Keeps the screen on during active training sessions so the color flash cue remains visible.
  • MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS — Allows the App to adjust audio routing as needed for accurate microphone input.
  • INTERNET — Required to load advertisements via AdMob.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — Allows AdMob to check network availability before requesting ads.

No other permissions are requested or required.

5. Data Storage

Training session history, calibration data, and all user preferences are stored in a local database on your device. We have no access to this data. You can delete it at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing the App's data through your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 13 and older and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
